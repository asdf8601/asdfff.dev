# Mathematical Formulas and Matrices

## Inline Math

The quadratic formula is $x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}$ and Euler's identity is $e^{i\pi} + 1 = 0$.

## Display Math

The Pythagorean theorem:

$$
a^2 + b^2 = c^2
$$

## Summation and Integration

Sum of first $n$ natural numbers:

$$
\sum_{i=1}^{n} i = \frac{n(n+1)}{2}
$$

Fundamental theorem of calculus:

$$
\int_{a}^{b} f(x) \, dx = F(b) - F(a)
$$

## Matrices

Identity matrix:

$$
I = \begin{pmatrix}
1 & 0 & 0 \\
0 & 1 & 0 \\
0 & 0 & 1
\end{pmatrix}
$$

Matrix multiplication:

$$
\begin{pmatrix}
a & b \\
c & d
\end{pmatrix}
\begin{pmatrix}
x \\
y
\end{pmatrix}
=
\begin{pmatrix}
ax + by \\
cx + dy
\end{pmatrix}
$$

## Determinant

$$
\det(A) = \begin{vmatrix}
a & b & c \\
d & e & f \\
g & h & i
\end{vmatrix}
= a\begin{vmatrix}
e & f \\
h & i
\end{vmatrix}
- b\begin{vmatrix}
d & f \\
g & i
\end{vmatrix}
+ c\begin{vmatrix}
d & e \\
g & h
\end{vmatrix}
$$

## Systems of Linear Equations

$$
\begin{cases}
2x + 3y = 7 \\
x - y = 1
\end{cases}
$$

## Greek Letters and Symbols

Common symbols: $\alpha, \beta, \gamma, \Delta, \Sigma, \Omega$

$$
\lim_{x \to \infty} \frac{1}{x} = 0
$$

## Fractions and Binomials

Binomial coefficient:

$$
\binom{n}{k} = \frac{n!}{k!(n-k)!}
$$

## Complex Expressions

The normal distribution probability density function:

$$
f(x) = \frac{1}{\sigma\sqrt{2\pi}} e^{-\frac{1}{2}\left(\frac{x-\mu}{\sigma}\right)^2}
$$

## Eigenvalues

For a matrix $A$, eigenvalues $\lambda$ satisfy:

$$
\det(A - \lambda I) = 0
$$

Eigenvectors satisfy:

$$
Av = \lambda v
$$
